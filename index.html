Our Promise To You

By collecting merchandise donated from the community along with purchasing merchandise from wholesalers and charitable organizations, Value Village thrift stores are able to provide a wide array of items, and unlike other discount outlets, we price them all individually to reflect their condition. This means that when you shop at a Value Village thrift store anywhere throughout Atlanta, you’re getting exactly what you pay for and at the best value possible!

Sustainable Fashion, Sustainable Lifestyle About Our Stores

As 97% of our thrift store profits go to the American Kidney Services, we are consistently giving back to the communities in which we do business. Value Village thrift stores also promote eco-friendly living by selling pre-used clothing and other merchandise, and then recycling any items are not suitable for sale. So, when you shop at Value Village thrift stores, not only are you saving valuable dollars on clothing for your family and furnishings for your home, you’re also adopting a greener lifestyle.

We guarantee that you will fall in love with our trendy fashion & home goods. Join us for your thrift shopping adventures or simply to just browse the furniture for future DIY projects!

*Note: We accept donations for every category listed under items we sell with the exception of matresses. Please visit call your local store for information regarding large furniture.
